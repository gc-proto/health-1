Low risk of Zika virus

The mosquito that carries the Zika virus is present in this area, but there have been no documented cases in the last 12 months.

Travellers are advised to take precautions against mosquito bites.

High risk of Zika virus

Zika virus has been reported in this country within the last 12 months. Pregnant women, or couples planning a pregnancy should avoid travel to this area.
